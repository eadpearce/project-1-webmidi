Instructions

You can play the piano with the mouse/touchscreen or by using the keys A to J for white notes and W to U for black notes.

Simon Says:

Press the play button and the computer will play a sequence of notes. Try to play the sequence back correctly. Hard mode means the keyboard will not light up as the computer plays but doubles the points you earn for a correct answer. Turning notation on or off does not affect points earned.

Piano Hero:

Press the play button and a sequence of notes will scroll along the manuscript above. Play the correct note on the keyboard as it reaches the cursor. As you progress to the next stage the notes will get faster!
